The Digital Peace System

Why "Just Try Harder" Fails

The Old Way

Willpower

  • Parent: "Turn it off because I said so."
  • Child: "Why? That's not fair!"
  • Result: Decision fatigue & screaming.
The System

Rhythm

  • Parent: "The wifi sleeps at 7 PM."
  • Child: Accepts the environment.
  • Result: Peace, Predictability & Sleep.

Goal: Move from Policing (You vs. Them) to Predictability (Them vs. Clock)

The Science

Crowding Out

We don't "ban" screens. We fill the jar with "Big Rocks" first: Sleep, Connection, and Movement. Screen time fits harmlessly in the spaces left over.

Predictability

Anxiety stems from the unknown. When screen time is rhythmic (same time, same place), the addiction loop breaks because the child knows exactly when their "fix" is coming.

The Family Rhythm Framework

Customize these hours to fit your family needs.

RED LIGHT: The "No Fly" Zones

Bedrooms • Dinner Table • 1 Hour Before Bed

Non-negotiable. Screens in bedrooms are the #1 cause of sleep deprivation. We protect sleep sovereignty at all costs.

YELLOW LIGHT: The Conditional Zones

Living Room • Kitchen (After "The Big 3")

Screen time is a Result, not a Default. It is available only after: 1. Chores, 2. Homework, 3. Outside Play.

GREEN LIGHT: The Free Zones

After School • Weekends • Movie Nights

Let them play! Once responsibilities are met, tech is a healthy tool for fun, creativity, and connection.
