Critical Failure Analysis

Why do 90% of info-product businesses fail? Understanding these pitfalls is the first step to mitigation.

Failure Attribution Model

1. Distribution Failure (40%)

The Trap: Spending weeks creating the "perfect" guide but having zero audience.
The Fix: Validate *before* building. Use Pinterest/TikTok to gauge interest in the topic first.

2. Trust Deficit (25%)

The Trap: "Who are you to tell me how to raise my child?"
The Fix: Use NotebookLM to source expert content. Add "Curated by [Name]" not "Written by". Transparency wins.

3. Platform Dependency

The Trap: Relying 100% on Gumroad or Etsy.
The Fix: Collect emails immediately. An email list is the only asset you truly own.
